Born from the Mediterranean.
Built to last.

Palermo Bleu is a Barcelona-based clothing brand rooted in the visual language of European expressionist painting — the raw pigment of Rothko, the gestural energy of Basquiat, the colour field of the Mediterranean coast at dusk. We believe clothing should carry that same sense of history and intention.

Our garments are designed with a single obsession: cut and proportion. Every piece is developed to enhance the body's natural silhouette — broader shoulders, a cleaner chest line, a length that neither swallows nor exposes. The result is clothing that simply fits better and makes you look better, without requiring you to think about it.

We work with local suppliers and producers in and around Barcelona, keeping our supply chain short, transparent, and accountable. Fabrics are sourced for quality and durability — natural fibres, honest weights, no synthetic shortcuts. We produce in small quantities, on purpose.

Sustainability for us isn't a marketing claim. It's the consequence of making fewer, better things — garments that don't fall apart, don't go out of style, and don't end up in landfill after a season. Pieces you keep.

The Mediterranean informs everything: the warmth of terracotta, the blue of open water, the unhurried confidence of someone who grew up knowing how to dress without trying too hard. That's the world Palermo Bleu lives in.
